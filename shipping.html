🚚 Shipping & Cancellation Policy

Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with Perlyn Beauty.

Orders are processed and dispatched within 1–3 business days after confirmation.

Delivery typically takes 3–8 business days from the date of dispatch, depending on your location.

All orders are shipped through trusted courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery.

Once shipped, customers will receive tracking details via email or SMS (if available).

Perlyn Beauty is not responsible for delays caused by couriers, weather conditions, or events beyond our control.

Cancellation Policy

At Perlyn Beauty, we want every shopping experience to be smooth and satisfying. However, we understand that you may occasionally need to cancel an order. Please review our cancellation policy below.

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase.

To cancel, please email us at perlynbeauty@gmail.com within this timeframe and include your Order ID and reason for cancellation.

No Cancellation After Dispatch

Once your order has been dispatched, we are unable to cancel or modify it, as it will already be in transit with our courier partner.

Refund for Cancelled Orders

If your cancellation request is approved, your refund will be processed within 5–8 business days to the original payment method.

Refund timelines may vary slightly depending on your bank or payment provider.

For any queries or assistance, please contact our customer support team at perlynbeauty@gmail.com — we’re always here to help with honesty, care, and transparency.
